# ZeroTrace Disclaimer

**Applies to:** ZeroTrace Camera, ZeroTrace Vault, ZeroTrace Mail, ZeroTrace Chat, ZeroTrace Blog (collectively, the "ZeroTrace Apps")

## No Server, No Data Access

The ZeroTrace Apps operate on a fully peer-to-peer (P2P) and on-device architecture. There is no central ZeroTrace server that stores, processes, transmits, or has access to your content, files, messages, photos, or communications.

All encryption and decryption occurs locally on your device. Encryption keys are generated and stored on your device and are never transmitted to, or accessible by, ZeroTrace, its developer, or any third party.

As a result, the developer of ZeroTrace:

- Cannot access, view, read, or recover any user content
- Cannot decrypt any user data under any circumstance
- Does not store user data on any server
- Cannot respond to data requests, subpoenas, or government orders with user content, because no such content exists outside your device
